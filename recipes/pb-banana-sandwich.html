Peanut Butter and Banana Sandwich

Description

This is a super quick and simple lunch that is also filling. This sandwich was my go-to while I was in college.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 whole banana
  • 1 jar peanut butter

Steps

  1. Cover both slices of bread in as much peanut butter as you like. Be sure to spread it crust to crust. We do not want any non-buttered patches on the slices.
  2. Unpeel and cut the banana into dimes about a quarter inch thick. Place the cut banana dimes on one of the slices of bread.
  3. Take the other slice with no banana on it and place it atop of the banana'd slice.
  4. Viola! You've done it. Now go enjoy that ooey-gooey goodness. Be sure to have a glass of water in case you need to free your mouth from getting too peanut-buttery.

Tips

  • Optionally, you can toast the slices of bread to add an extra crunch to each bite you take.
  • For the sweet toothed among us, you can add a little honey to the combination for some extra sweetness.
